Understanding the Telltale Signs of Heat-Damaged Hair: Split Ends, Dryness, and Dullness

08 Dec 2023

We all love to experiment with our hair, whether it's through styling tools, blow-drying, or occasional trips to the salon. However, these styling practices, while giving us stunning looks, can sometimes take a toll on our precious locks. If you've noticed split ends, dryness, and dullness in your hair, it might be time to consider whether your hair is suffering from heat damage. Split Ends: One of the most common signs of heat damage is the appearance of split ends. High temperatures from styling tools can cause the protective outer layer of your hair, known as the cuticle, to crack and split. This leads to frayed ends that weaken the hair shaft and make it more prone to breakage.
